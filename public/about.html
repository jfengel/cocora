About Cocora

Cocora is a COVID-19 Compliance Ratings App. People can submit their review:

Pretty good compliance (almost everybody is wearing a mask correctly) and staying six feet apart.

Not great, but most people are at least are making an attempt.

Bad. A lot of people unmasked, or wearing them wrong.

Wearing masks and keeping apart saves lives. Yours, others, and people you care about. The only way to stop COVID-19 is to prevent it from spreading. That means keeping yourself from getting it, and avoiding spreading it yourself -- which you can do even if you don't have symptoms.

Cocora is based on user input. It can help you pick where to go to be safer. We try to keep the data quality good, but use your common sense: wear a mask, keep your distance, and if people aren't complying, go some place else.

Privacy policy

Cocora stores the ratings that you send to us and the locations that were rated, along with your user identifier and the date/time it was stored. We use that information to calculate an average rating for a location. We publish only the aggregate, not your individual ratings. Individual ratings are accessbile only by the server.

Users can use Cocora without logging in. Logins are required only for ratings.

We require logins for ratings to try to keep data quality higher. We do not sell it, share it, or send you emails. We do not record your name or email. Logins are required solely to keep ratings to one per person, per site, per day.

Be aware that your user identifier is unique to you, so our data contains a list of places you have been, which is potentially sensitive. That data is kept privately on Cocora's servers, but while we try to protect your data, we cannot absolutely guarantee security. If you have any concerns about your personal safety, don't submit any ratings.
