About Cocora

Cocora is a COVID-19 Compliance Ratings App. People can submit their review:

Pretty good compliance (almost everybody is wearing a mask correctly) and staying six feet apart.

Not great, but most people are at least are making an attempt.

Bad. A lot of people unmasked, or wearing them wrong.

Wearing masks and keeping apart saves lives. Yours, others, and people you care about. The only way to stop COVID-19 is to prevent it from spreading. That means keeping yourself from getting it, and avoiding spreading it yourself -- which you can do even if you don't have symptoms.

Cocora is based on user input. It can help you pick where to go to be safer. We try to keep the data quality good, but use your common sense: wear a mask, keep your distance, and if people aren't complying, go some place else.

Privacy policy

We require logins for ratings to try to keep data quality higher. We don't do anything with your data except to work out the average score. We don't sell it, share it, or send you emails. Logins are required solely to keep ratings to one per person, per site, per day.

We don't record your name or email or anything else, just an ID, the location, and the rating. We try to protect your data, but can't absolutely guarantee security. If you have any concerns about your personal safety, don't submit any ratings.
